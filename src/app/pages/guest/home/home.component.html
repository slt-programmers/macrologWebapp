Track all the things

Whatever your goal is, the bottom line in changing your body is simple. To lose weight you consume less energy than you burn, and to gain weight you eat more than you burn. How do you know what you're doing is effective? Track it! Track your methods, track your progress, track all the things. Macrolog can help you keep an overview of the food you eat, your activities and your progress in terms of weight. The purpose is to collect data, and see what works for you.

Macronutrients

Part of tracking everything is also tracking the proportions in which you eat. Macrolog, as the name suggests, is more focussed on tracking macronutrients rather than just calories. We won't pretend to know much about dieting itself, and what works for one person may not work for another. But we know proteins do something else to your body than fat does, and fat acts differently from carbohydrates. Not tracking macronutrients would be neglecting data that could play a huge role in accomplishing your physical goal. It should be the basis rather than an extra feature.

Simple and quick...

We think time spend tracking food is time you're not spending on the things you love. Logging food should therefore be as easy and quick as possible, without getting inaccurate. Macrolog will help you with this, but how?

Just your products

First off, Macrolog's food list only contains products that you personally use. Other apps are hooked up to large databases where other users can add to. This sometimes clutters the list and it can be hard to pick the right product. Have you ever tried to find the right type of bread amongst the 100 types to pick from? Macrolog's list starts out empty, and grows over time while you add entries to your diary. You will never be overwhelmed by the amount of products to pick from. It's just your favorite foods.

Portions

Second, Macrolog lets you define portions for your products. If you always have the same amount of oats for breakfast, you're only gonna enter that quantity once. You can add multiple portions to every food, in case you eat varying amounts. This speeds up adding food to your diary.

Dishes

But we didn't stop there. If you always have the same amount of oats, yogurt and honey every morning, you're only gonna want to enter one thing, your breakfast. This is why Macrolog enables you to define dishes and add all the ingredients at once. These ingredient amounts are then fully adjustable, just in case you needed an extra spoon of honey.

... and complex and dynamic

While the logging must be as simple and easy as possible, the things we want to do with the data are more complex. It's nice to see if you reach your daily goals, but you also might want to look at your weekly, monthly, or even yearly accomplishments. If you track your eating and exercising habbits for a longer period of time you are able to make predictions for the future, should you stick with it. Macrolog offers tools to make these aspects visible to you.

Dynamically adjusting goals

When your body changes, your need for energy changes. When you weigh less, you also need less energy daily. And when you've bulked up, you need more energy to sustain that amount of muscle. Macrolog enables you to automatically adjust your daily intake goal based on your new physique. This prevents plateauing, which is something many people eventually experience when they don't adjust their eating habbits to their new bodily needs.

When is Macrolog for you?

When you value macronutrients over calories. When you want things clear and simple. When you know what you want to eat. When you don't need advice. When you want something other than just losing weight. When you like analyzing your data. When you like full control.
