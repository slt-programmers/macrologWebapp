About Macrolog

Macrolog was developed by two software engineers who wanted to get in shape. After using several food tracking applications we got a little tired of adds and payed functions which, in our opinion, should be free. So for laughs and learning we decided to take it upon ourselves to build a better app.

Macrolog heavily focusses on counting macronutrients instead of counting calories. We believe that it is healthier and more effective to exactly know what you are eating. This will better help you in accomplishing your pysical goals than just tracking calories. Now, the concept of the "right" proportions is somewhat debatable, and what works for one person may not work for another. We won't pretend to know much about dieting, so during the intake a few calculations and suggestions based on mainstream theory will be done. These suggestion are entirely free to ignore.

In the end, we just want to help you measure.
